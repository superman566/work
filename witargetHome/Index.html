WELCOME

 SHUXIN® has been considered as one of the finest and most celebrated patisseries in Tunderbay. SHUXIN®  prides itself on creating the freshet and finest handmade cakes. Its pastry chefs follow recipes that have been refined over the years to provide the highest quality in taste and appearance. The special care and attention in creating private clientele.

        

FOUNDER

SHUXIN® was founded by Shuxin Lei in 2003. Lei is from China. Her family owns Wasabi Bistro, a restaurant group with locations in Shanghai, China. Lei's inspiration for SHUXIN grew from her travels throughout Hongkong. After gruaduating from business school, Lei took over SHUXIN. She also studied pasty at the International Culinary Center.
